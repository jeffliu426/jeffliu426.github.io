Jeffrey Liu

Jeffrey is a proud UC Berkeley graduate who studied computer science and economics. His combination of technical skills and interest in helping tackle social issues has led him to several research positions at Berkeley, particularly within the Berkeley Institute of Data Science, CITRIS Social Apps lab, and Pacific Research Platform. Jeffrey has also held several software engineering internships, most recently at LendingClub where he contributed to internal software tools that helped other software engineers work more efficiently by automating certain processes. In his spare time, Jeffrey loves to play basketball and follow sports in general.
