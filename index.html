Jeffrey Liu

Jeffrey is a proud UC Berkeley graduate who double majored in computer science and economics. His technical skills and interest in tackling social issues has led him to several research positions at Berkeley, within the Berkeley Institute of Data Science, CITRIS Social Apps lab, and Pacific Research Platform. Jeffrey has held several software engineering internships, most recently at LendingClub where he worked on software tools that helped other software engineers work more efficiently via automation. In his spare time, Jeffrey loves to imagine traveling the world (sometimes he does!) and play basketball. You can often find him at a Giants baseball game eating garlic fries and a hot link. Feel free to reach out to him about any potential job opportunities or if you'd just like to chat.
